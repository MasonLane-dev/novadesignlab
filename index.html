About Me

Cardiff-Based Freelance Web Designer for Local Businesses

I'm Mason — a freelance web designer based in Cardiff. I started NovaDesignLab because I noticed something: so many great local businesses in Cardiff either have no website at all, or they're stuck with an outdated one that doesn't do their work justice.

I'm relatively new to the industry, but that means every project gets my full attention. I don't use generic templates or rush through work. I take the time to understand your business, your customers, and what you want to achieve — then I build a website that actually helps you grow.

I specialise in affordable, conversion-focused websites for independent businesses — plumbers, cafes, gyms, salons, real estate agents, and more. If you run a local business in Cardiff, Penarth, Newport, or Barry, I'd love to hear from you.
